What Makes Color Important

Color plays a vitally important role in the world in which we live. Color can be portrayed as thinking, Change of actions, and can cause reactions. It is a form of communication, meaning that color is irreplaceable. Red means "stop" and green means "go" hence the reason why Traffic Lights send this universal message. The color used in products, web site, business card, or logo will cause powerful reactions. without color nothing would exist today, hence the reason why color is still around.
